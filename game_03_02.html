  • Synopsis

    Deploy early into an unprecedented take on WW2. Pre-order Battlefield V and get the Enlister Offer which gives you early access to the Battlefield V open beta. Pre-ordering also gives you in-game benefits, such as soldier customization options and access to special assignments starting at launch week. Enter mankind's greatest conflict with Battlefield V as the series goes back to its roots. Take on physical, all-out multiplayer with your squad in vast Grand Operations and cooperative Combined Arms modes, or witness human drama set against global combat in single-player War Stories. As you fight in epic, unexpected locations across the globe, enjoy a rich and immersive battlefield.

    Battlefield V pre-order Enlister Offer highlights*:
    Early access to the Battlefield V beta - Try Battlefield V beforehand through early access to the open beta. Be one of the first on the front lines, and witness Battlefield and WW2 as you've never seen before

    Tides of war - Embark on a journey where new content drops continuously transport you and your company to a huge variety of key WW2 locations, where new battlefields and gear await
